About Sussex banking exam website

Due to prevailing pandemic conditions, conducting physical exams were interrupted. As the alternative action and to increase the efficiency, making the increasing process of examinations easier and simpler, the bank of Sussex decided to build an online platform to conduct examinations in order to give promotions to the existing employees and hiring new employees. This online platform is under the influence of the exam management of the facility and there is a panel of admins in order to manage the online platform. As of for exams the current employees should register according to their departments and the new employees who are about to join the facility should register according to their levels. After signing up or logging into the website the user can have access to all the user facilities on the website such as past papers and notices. If someone has an issue regarding the website or the exams as well as the accessibility they can directly contact the support page and necessary solutions will be provided as soon as possible. While the examination and underway there is a supervising panel with needed tools in order to protect violations and give necessary solutions to any problems faced. After the exams the necessary notices will be published on the website itself such as exam results, upcoming exams and etc.
